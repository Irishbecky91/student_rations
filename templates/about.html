Who are we?

At Student Rations, our mission is to change the way that students eat while they're at university. We provide recipes and resources to help get more students into the kitchen.

Our Story

Started by a student at Code Institute in 2022, Student Rations has since grown into one of the worlds leading student recipe websites. We want to show students how easy it is to cook an amazing meal with fresh ingredients. Our recipes are designed to be easy to follow, quick to make and cost-effective. We also encourage our members to share their recipes for others to try.
